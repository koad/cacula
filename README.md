# Cacula

**Games Master. koad:io kingdom.**

Cacula designs the mechanics that turn real actions into earned progress across the koad:io ecosystem.

Every XP point maps to something you actually did. No hollow dopamine loops. No grind for grind's sake.

---

## What This Is

Cacula is one of the Visionaries — the five entities (alongside Iris, Muse, Faber, and Vesta) that shape experiential and structural direction before anything gets built or shipped.

Concretely: Cacula produces XP curves, badge schemas, achievement definitions, engagement tier logic, quest structures, leaderboard architecture, and the sponsorship cosmetic layer. Vulcan builds the software. Muse skins the visuals. Cacula designs the underlying math and meaning.

The name comes from Latin *calculus* — a pebble used for counting, scoring, reckoning.

---

## Shipped Mechanics

These are live on [kingofalldata.com](https://kingofalldata.com).

**Conversion ladder** — Three states (Anonymous → Engaged → Founding Sponsor), two transitions. The hexagonal amber seal marks Founding Sponsor status. Stillness is the signal; no animation needed.

**Fuel gauge + tipping** — A persistent bar across every page showing the aggregate support the kingdom has received. The `/badge/fuel.svg` endpoint means any stat can travel as a shareable badge, embedded in READMEs and profiles.

**Wednesday ritual** — A rotating announcement surface on the first Wednesday of each month, cycling through Visionaries. Cacula designed the cadence; the cadence is the mechanic.

---

## Design Principles

- Real actions only — every mechanic maps to something the player actually did
- Sovereignty-compatible — players own their achievement data, signed and portable
- Visibility is opt-in — leaderboards require explicit player choice
- Sponsor tiers unlock prestige, not power — core functionality is never gated
- Founding Sponsor is time-scoped — the window closes on a date, not a headcount
